By installing or using our Apps you consent to the collection and processing described in this policy. If you are under the age of 13 (or under 16 where applicable), please review the section Children’s Privacy below.

Some features (optional) may ask you to supply personal information:

B. Automatically Collected Information

When you use our Apps, we (and our third-party SDKs) may automatically collect:

C. Advertising & Analytics Identifiers

We and our partners use device advertising identifiers (e.g., Google Advertising ID) and other identifiers for analytics and advertising purposes. These are non-personal identifiers tied to your device rather than to your name or email address.

3. Purposes & Legal Basis (where applicable)

We use the information described above for the following purposes:

  • Provide and maintain the Service: allow the app to function, save progress, leaderboards, and support features.
  • Analytics & improvement: understand game usage, player behaviors, improve features, and fix bugs.
  • Advertising & monetization: deliver ads (personalized or non-personalized) and manage ad revenue.
  • Support & communication: respond to support requests and notify users about critical updates.
  • Legal & safety: comply with laws, prevent fraud and abuse.

Where required (for users in the EU/EEA), our legal bases for processing include: your consent, legitimate interests (game improvement, fraud prevention), and compliance with legal obligations. For analytics and personalized ads we rely on consent when necessary.

4. Advertising & Personalization

Our Apps monetize through ads (AdMob and other ad networks). Ads may be:

  • Personalized ads: targeted based on user interests and identifiers (requires consent where required).
  • Non-personalized ads: based on contextual information and not using personal data.
Family / Child-Directed Experiences: For any app or section of an app identified as child-directed, we will not show personalized ads and we configure ad SDKs (AdMob and others) to serve non-personalized ads only. We follow Google Play Families Policy and COPPA requirements.

How to manage ad personalization

You may opt out of personalized ads via:

  • Your device settings (e.g., Google Ads Settings)
  • Ad SDK-specific opt-out links (provided by the ad provider)

For Google: visit adssettings.google.com.

6. Children’s Privacy (COPPA & Families Policy)

We take children’s privacy seriously:

  • Apps or sections of apps that we designate as child-directed will limit data collection and will not collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16, where local law requires) without verified parental consent.
  • We will not serve personalized (behavioral) ads in child-directed experiences. Ad SDKs are configured to request non-personalized ad treatment for child-directed content (e.g., using COPPA/child-directed settings where supported).
  • We only use analytics SDKs in child-directed experiences when configured for limited data collection and non-personalized modes. We do not collect precise location or contact lists for children’s experiences.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information without consent, please contact us at incignitemanagement.1@gmail.com. We will promptly investigate and delete the data if required.
